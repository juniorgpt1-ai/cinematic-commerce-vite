# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Consumers in Belo Horizonte buying Grupo Boticário and Eudora fragrances and haircare products for themselves or as gifts. They value convenience and speed — they want premium products without the friction of going to a physical store. They're WhatsApp-native and comfortable making purchasing decisions through conversation rather than a traditional shopping cart.

## Product Purpose

Convert visitors into WhatsApp orders for premium fragrances and haircare with VIP 1-hour delivery via Uber Flash in Belo Horizonte. The site funnels every interaction toward the WhatsApp CTA: a fast, human, low-friction purchase channel that replaces the traditional e-commerce cart.

## Positioning

Get premium Grupo Boticário fragrances delivered in 1 hour — skip the store entirely. Maison Fragrance is the fastest way to buy Boticário and Eudora in Belo Horizonte, combining instant WhatsApp ordering with curated product selection.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: WhatsApp order with VIP 1-hour delivery via Uber Flash
- Secondary CTA: Free WhatsApp consultation — ask questions, get recommendations, no commitment
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: The fastest way to get Boticário and Eudora fragrances delivered in BH — WhatsApp, 1 hour, done.
- Belief ladder:
  1. These are genuine Grupo Boticário and Eudora products (authenticity)
  2. Delivery really happens in 1 hour (speed promise)
  3. The price is fair and worth the convenience (value)
  4. Ordering via WhatsApp is easy, personal, and safe (trust)
- Proof on hand: None yet. The business is new or hasn't collected testimonials, reviews, or press. The partner brand logos (Boticário, Eudora, OUI, Vult, Quem Disse Berenice?, Multi B) serve as the primary trust signal in the absence of customer proof.

## Brand Personality

Warm, approachable, familiar. Luxury that feels like home, not like a boutique with a velvet rope. The voice is confident but not formal, premium but not distant, personal but not pushy. The emotional goal is trust and ease — a visitor should feel like they're texting a well-connected friend who knows fragrances, not filling out a cold order form.

## Anti-references

Mass-market marketplace aesthetics — Mercado Livre product pages, iFood-style UI, bright primary colors, dense grids, aggressive badges, and promotional noise. Nothing that reads as a generic e-commerce template or a discount-driven marketplace. The experience should feel curated and calm, not like a digital shelf with 10,000 SKUs.

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth over formality.** Approachable elegance — premium quality without cold distance or intimidation.
2. **Speed is the product.** One-hour delivery isn't an afterthought; it's the headline every section reinforces.
3. **Calm curation, not marketplace noise.** A thoughtfully selected catalog presented with breathing room. Fewer products, more intention.
4. **Conversation, not transaction.** WhatsApp is a human channel. The design should feel like the start of a personal relationship, not a checkout funnel.
5. **Genuine, not aspirational.** The brand doesn't pretend to be Parisian haute parfumerie. It's a real business in Belo Horizonte offering real products with real speed. Authenticity beats borrowed prestige.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Color contrast ratios must meet 4.5:1 for body text and 3:1 for large text. Reduced motion support for all animations. The site is in Brazilian Portuguese — copy should feel natural and colloquial, not machine-translated or overly formal.
